Christopher Nolan

Christopher Nolan is an award-winning film director, producer, and screenwriter. His films have grossed over $5 billion worldwide and he has been nominated for 11 Academy awards among his repertoire of 36 major award nominations. He has gained international recognition and respect for his 12 films spanning different genres and being made across four decades. His films have been hailed by critics as being incredibly inventive and cognitive. His films deal with many intangible and larger than life concepts that include human morality, the concept of time, and other metaphysical themes. Some of his most well-known works include The Dark Knight (2008), Inception (2010), and Dunkirk (2017).
